The Wise Piggy Wallet

Piggy Wallet, where you can set a goal to save how much money in a certain period, the application will calculate how much money you need to save each day, in addition to out with Piggy Wallet app, you can play mini-games (mini-games are a model of games that advertise in partnership with brands and businesses), with playing mini-games every day (limited number of plays per day) you will accumulate Piggy Wallet coins. Piggy Wallet coin is a virtual value that can be earned from money transfer, or payment, can be bought with money or earned from mini games, from the number of kilometers gained from running ... Piggy Wallet coins cannot be withdrawn and cannot be paid, but they can be used to exchange for discount coupons and especially contribute a large part to charity funds. Therefore, in our opinion, with the presence of Piggy Wallet, you will be easier to manage their spending, the presence of Piggy Wallet has great human value, helping social issues, it not only stops at personal benefits but also has a positive impact on the community.
